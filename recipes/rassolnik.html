Rassolnik

Description

Rassolnik is a traditional russian recipe that I learned how to make when I was living in Belgium. I absolutely love this soup and tend to add more pickles as thats my favorite. See below for the ingredients list

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds of stew beef
  • 1/2 cabbage
  • Carrots
  • 1 large onion
  • 4 potatoes
  • Barley

How to make

First I boil the water and add in the beef. As the beef is cooking I shave off carrots, cut the cabbage to strips. I cut up the potatoes into small squares and add them to the stew.

I add the carrots and cabbage to a pan and cook. Once the cabbage and carrots are cooked I add them to the beef stew. I then add my chopped pickles and a 2 cups of pickle juice. I leave it cooking for 4 hours on low heat. Then enjoy with a dallop of daisy sour cream. Very good and easy to make.
