Spaghetti

Description

This is my favorite meal! I make it a little too often to be honest. But its good.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti noodles (preferably Ramos)
  • Primal Kitchen tomato and Basil sauce
  • Caldo de Tomate poweder
  • Ground Turkey 90% lean
How to make
  1. Boil water
  2. Once water is boiled add the spaghetti
  3. Wait 10-15 minutes and then strain the water from the noodles
  4. Cook turkey and then add in totatoe sauce, and tomatoe powder about two spoonfuls
  5. Add in the spaghetti

Bon Appetit!!! I hope you enjoy this delicious simple and quick spaghetti recipe.
